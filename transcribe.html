What to do?

There are basically two ways of sending in your file(s) for transcription.

1. Simply fill out the form below, attach your "EDITABLE" PDF file(s), and hit the "SUBMIT" button.

2. Click on the "WHATSAPP" button to send your file(s) across, giving you direct access to easy communication with us.

Average transcription duration is two(2) to three(3) days for a five(5) page sheet music, to allow scrutiny and proper inspection before submission

About Us

We are a team of classical musicians, seeking to solve the problem of inability to sight read amongst choristers and budding musicians. We make it easy for you to play and, or sing your favourite piece, boycotting the numerous years of sight reading study.
The world as we know, is in an era where working isn't necessarily hard, but rather smart, and we offer an innovative way to work and play smart not necessarily hard.
